BonAHNSa is funded out of pocket from Dennogin, but is supplemented by generous donors who want to keep the server alive.

Donating is not needed for its continued operation, nor will you gain any sort of benefits on BonAHNSa, but is absolutely appreciated.

Some information about donations:
  • Funds are all handled by the provider, NFO. Each dollar goes into a coffer which cannot be withdrawn from and can only be used to fund the server's uptime. Frankly speaking, each donation goes solely to the server and CAN NOT be taken by any of the admins/operators.
  • The monthly cost of server uptime is $38.79, plus the additional dollar for the BonAHNSa domain name. This is the cost for both the public and competitive server.
  • The minimum amount you can donate is $5 (sorry!)
  • You can leave a message which will be put in the list below. Leaving your ingame name will also be appreciated, otherwise I will consider it an anonymous donation.
  • Donating will NOT give you any sort of privilege or adminship. If you would like admin, you only have to ask one of the current admins (listed on the homepage)
