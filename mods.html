Modifications


This page has modifications which will enhance your NT experience/gameplay, from configs and texture changes to crosshairs and binds. They are not cheats, so you won't get banned/VAC'd for using them.


Configs

An old config, but it has several improvements over a vanilla install. Be aware that it is a config file for performance, so CVARs such as r_shadows will be set to 0, which may or may not impact gameplay.

If you (for some godforsaken reason) want to play with a gamepad, this config may be for you.

Not exactly a config, but it will make the hl2.exe Large Address Aware (LAA), basically meaning it can access more memory/RAM, thereby improving stability, performance, and allowing you to run the game on high texture settings.

A guide on importing sprays into NT. Has a few extra steps as opposed to other Source games. As of writing, only BonAHNSa and Creamy have sprays enabled.

FOV Fixes

These DLLs should be downloaded with the understanding that they are still being developed by Kudegra and Lili, and may be buggy/janky. ALWAYS back up your vanilla .dll file BEFORE installing.
It is also highly recommended to install the NTCore 4gb Patch when using these DLLs, "Shit can get fucky wucky with unpatched even on medium..."- Kudegra.
To install, replace client.dll in [/NEOTOKYO/NeotokyoSource/bin] with the .dll in the downloaded .zip file.

Kudegra's fixed 90 DLL sets the ingame FOV to 90, with no option for adjustment. There may be some viewmodel animation bugs, but they're worth the better FOV.

Lili's DLL that allows the FOV console command to work. Allows for any FOV configuration, with the side effect of your mouse sensitivity changing with the FOV.

Scripts

An aiming script that allows you to hold down M2 to aim as opposed to pressing it once and continuing to aim.

Pressing 4 will now allow you to swap between frags, smokes and dets. No more will you (accidentally) toss a smoke down only for it to be a frag and instagib your entire team.

You can now press Q or E once to lean in their respective directions. No more will you have to hold them down as you're trying to strafe.

Simply press CTRL and you will stay crouched until you press it again.

Customization

A guide on changing out HUD elements. Goes on plenty of tangents but should get you started on making the HUD work better for you.

Changes out the star (icons over friendly teammates) texture to something more visible and more kawaii. Install instructions included.

A pack of crosshairs, of various types, colors, shapes and sizes.

A simple program that allows you to choose a variety of crosshairs Place in your root NEOTOKYO directory.

VGUI pack of new ghost beacon, cap zone, and white dot crosshair. Sakhan's own setup.

Textures which change out a bunch of the jumbotron screens to show off different ads, memes, and outdated in-jokes. Not for the feint of heart, or immersionwhores.

Changes out the porn signs to show more skin.

Changes your background to an in-game render of Marketa, akin to HL2 main menu backgrounds. Also precaches all the player/weapon models so load times are improved. If you do not want to replace your autoexec config, simply add [map_background nt_background_ctg] to your existing config.

A set of fonts and config files for your HUD. Makes it look chic.

Reskins

Changes the Jinrai playermodels to the pre-Steam textures, otherwise known as 'Watermelon Jinrai'.

A variety of ZR reskins that work with the ZR68c, ZR68s, and ZR68l. Also comes with the vanilla texture in case you change your mind, or if they provide too much of a tacticool advantage in a competitive setting.

Swaps the shortbus icon to the cybertruck, in the event you fall off the roof on Skyline and hit the pavement harder than a rock thrown at a car window.

Swaps the RDS sight on the MX-S to the knockoff ACOG scope found on the vanilla MX.

Changes the ZR68 holosight to have a lil' denno follow you around on your adventures through NEOTOKYO. Warranty void if you die. Stick inside NeotokyoSource\materials\models\weapons

A pack full of pre-2013 patch weapons. Includes models, textures, animations and sounds, as well as a few modded in textures courtesy of Wahaha.

If you think the milso being internaly silenced is a little outlandish (We do too!), this mod is for you.

A pack scrapped together by Waha and Denno after finding some old NT footage of the sound designer, Filterdecay, testing out weapon sounds.

Tired of mixing up your smokes and frags? Now you can easily identify what nade you are holding at a glance.
